Technology Island

I grew up with the internet being already extremely advanced with all kinds of software available, so of course that means I played around with a bunch of them. I love learning about computers and exploring strange programs on the interwebs. Many programs allowed me to explore my creativity, I have video recording software, video editing software, and audio editing software, that I mess around with from time to time. I also have image, and video compression software, so I could get more storage. I didn't exclusively mess around with software, but also hardware, I love tinkering with broken electronic devices around the house when I could get my hands on them. One time I needed to replace the battery in my laptop because it was no longer able to hold its charge, so I searched for youtube videos, but couldn't find any useful ones because the tutorial/guide needed to be my exact laptop, I found Ifixit. With the guide from Ifixit I was able to find the manufacturer's website to buy the replacement battery and also replace the battery in my laptop. I love computers, I love software, and of course, I especially love it when they blend.
